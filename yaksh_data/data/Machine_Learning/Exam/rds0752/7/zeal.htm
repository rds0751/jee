About The Event

We all as children are very often told to think out of the box, learn new things, expand our vision and come out of just academics. Summer camp is considered as one of easiest way out for the same. Traditionally, various schools have been organizing summer camps emphasizing mainly on some sports activities and small scale workshops.

CoLinkers, the organizing team of ZEAL, focus on taking the concept of summer camp to the higher tier. We plan to incorporate mind-boggling workshops which would benefit the students in their future course academics as well as professional life. Inclusion of talks by various renowned personalities on topics like career, stress-management, entrepreneurship add an extra spark to the fire. Tours/educational visits is an another aspect we throw spotlight on as we believe in practicality and this would impart a huge level of practical knowledge to the students.

This would help you to understand yourself better and result in your overall development. We take pride in doing the same as its high time we innovate.
